Environment Club

About the Club

Club Secretary: Harsh M Kakasaniya

"Every Little Step Counts"

The Environment Club is the community of students from different disciplines concerned about environmental problems and wishes to do something about it. We conduct activities like Panel discussions and Young Reporters for Environment to raise awareness as well as certain fun events like Movie night with Eco-friendly bonfires and online Scavenger Hunt - The hunt for Sustainability. So, Come and join the Green Army of Ahmedabad University in the journey towards the Green and Clean Future.
