About the Club

Club Secretary: Radhika Bhavinbhai Contractor

"Art is an amalgamation of creativity and emotions"

The Fine Arts Club of the Ahmedabad University is the happy place for all creatives to explore. It is a platform where one explores the creative side of themselves and learns along with exploring the beautiful world of art. Be it Pictionary or Dream catcher or fluid art, you will always have a souvenir to take. It has always been treasure for the participants joining the Fine Arts Family.
